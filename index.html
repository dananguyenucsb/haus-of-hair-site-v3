Hau's of Hair Salon

At Hau's of Hair Salon, we are focused on complete customer satisfaction by providing superb quality work and outstanding customer service to our clients.

Our stylists have over 10 years experience in the hair industry and are well trained in the latest haircut and hair coloring techniques.

Hau's of Hair Salon is located in the beautiful Cambrian/Almaden neighborhood of San Jose, conveniently found next to Cup and Saucer Restaurant by Orchard Supply, inside Princeton Plaza.
